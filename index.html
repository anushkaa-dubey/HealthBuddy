Frequently Asked Questions

What is chronic disease management?

Chronic disease management involves ongoing care and support to help individuals manage their chronic health conditions, improve their quality of life, and reduce the risk of complications.

How can HealthBuddy help me?

HealthBuddy provides tools and resources to help you monitor your health, manage medications, schedule appointments, and access telehealth services, all in one place.

Is HealthBuddy free to use?

HealthBuddy offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic features, while the premium plan provides additional tools and services for comprehensive chronic disease management.

Health Facts

Fact 1

Regular physical activity can help prevent chronic diseases such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes, and some cancers.

Fact 2

Eating a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables can improve your overall health and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

Fact 3

Managing stress through activities like meditation, yoga, and deep breathing can improve your mental and physical health.
